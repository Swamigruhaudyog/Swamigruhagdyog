Our Story

Hello! I'm Supriya. Eight years ago, I had just one dream - to have my own small but self-sustaining business. And from this dream was born the brand - Swami Gruha Udyog. Since childhood, I've had a great passion for creating durable items from waste materials and handcrafted art... I've created with my own hands - wall pieces for decoration, torans, latkans, acrylic rangolis, puja plates, and even wedding rukhwats! Each design is a unique artwork that comes from my heart, made with love and crafted by hand. We've received wonderful responses from stalls in Mumbai, Pune, Borivali, Dadar, and Maharashtra Vyapari Peth. Not only that, but some customers have even sent our products to their children living in the USA. But then the question arose - how to make all this available to everyone in one place? So now... Swami Gruha Udyog is coming to you in digital form! You can easily contact us on Instagram, Facebook, WhatsApp, YouTube and our website, and place orders.
